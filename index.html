Administrative details

Our client is Solveig Olsen, CEO at CoffeeCorner, a local imaginary coffee shop located in Neverland. It is important that they deliver high quality coffee and tempting baked goods. They also sell coffee beans and chocolate.

Purpose

They wish to expand their clientele to also include customers online. Here they wish to sell homemade baked goods along with coffee, tea and hot chocolate. On the website, customers can pre-order food and drinks and then go to the store to pick it up. It is important that the website conveyss the importance of bringing your own cup or box, this will both be good for the environment and be cheaper for the customer.

Audience

Our intended audience are users that are too busy to wait in line and wish to pre-order. The audience will also include every citizen in Neverland, the coffee shop is not well established in the city yet so they wish to reach more people, both young and old. The younger would like that the website presents the coffee shop as an organic and environmentally friendly place while the elder would like the efficiency and plan focused part of the website.

The content of the site and how it is organized

It should have a picture of all products, the price, allergies and preferences, order size
Header: In the header Solveig wants a navigating panel with the following links: Home, Baked goods, Coffee and drinks, and About us.
Home: Solveig wants a tempting picture of baked goods and coffee, so any customer visiting the website would like to buy something. The name of the coffee shop, CoffeeCorner, will adorn the frontpage. Under the name, she wants a slogan.
Menu: This section will contain pictures of all the baked goods and drinks for sale at the CoffeeCorner, as well as the prices. It will also tell a few lines about the ingredients, and also if the food contains gluten, lactose, is vegetarian or vegan. All sorts of coffee, tea and hot chocolate will also be available at this site, and how big the size of the drink is. In addition the coffee beans will be available here.
About us: This section will contain information about the coffee shop, such as when it was established, who is the CEO and a little bit about their vision. In addition, the contact information will be provided here. The e-mail address and phone number will be links. There will also be a map showing the location of CoffeeCorner. The opening hours will be here as well.

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

  • Functional:
    • Solveg Olsen wants everyone to be able to order without making a profile
    • She also wants the customers to have the option to pay in Vipps or when they arrive at the shop
    • The website reflects the enioralmentlay friendly aspect of the place
    • The customers should be able to cancel anytime they want when ordering
    • he website should look modern and welcoming, to attract the younger generation. Solveig demands that the layout is user-friendly and reflects the cozy venues.
  • Non-functional
    • It is required that the Website should not load longer than 2 sec.
    • It should be easy to navigate on the website
    • Wish to sort the different types of drinks and food.

Final location

Solveig Olsen will donate the domain name and pay the hosting fees. Later the website will be found at www.CoffeeCorner.net.
